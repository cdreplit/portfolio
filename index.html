About Me

Hi my name is Chase! I’m a filmmaker and multimedia creator in San Francisco. I have been creating videos since I was four years old, filming travel documentaries and creative projects with friends.

Travel and geography deeply influence my work. Traveling all around the world and documenting my experiences has opened my perspective to all different cultures and backgrounds. I love capturing the moment, whether it’s trying food in a new country, reuniting with friends or getting the perfect highlight from a basketball game. I also like creating educational videos that are engaging and fun to watch.

Beyond filmmaking, I play and create my own music. My experience in drums and piano helps me create original soundtracks for my videos. By combining film and my original compositions, I’m able to build stories that feel cohesive and personal.

I’m excited to continue developing my voice as a filmmaker and musician, using both image and sound to tell stories.
